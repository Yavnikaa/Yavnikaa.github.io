About Us

We have been in the business of manufacturing and supplying quality polypropylene and low-density polythene bags to garment exporters since 1998. We work for varied brand names including Armani Exchange, Reebok, Levis, Mango, Marks & Spencer's, JC Penny, and DKNY. In our endeavour to put a curb on green house gases we have been working diligently with our associates in Chennai to produce biodegradable polybags of shelf life of approximately 2 years.

Infrastructure and Machinery

We have two manufacturing units in Gurgaon and IMT Manesar respectively. Our list of machinery include:
  • LDPE Extruders
  • PP Extruders
  • Flexographic Printing Machines
  • Side Sealing/Flap Making/Tape Attaching Machines
  • Bottom Sealing Machine
  • Hanger shaped sealing machine
  • DG Sets for power backup
The above machines are installed in our two facilities of 1200 sq yards each in Gurgaon and Manesar, run 24x7, to produce 80,000 bags per day.


Our Products

A non-exhaustive Product Portfolio is described as follows:
  • Recycled Claim Standard Polybags
  • Polypropylene/low density poly ethylene, plain/printed bags with self-adhesive tape
  • Bottom Sealed PP/LD bags with 1-4 colour printing
  • Side Sealed Bags with inside flap to hold the garment and thereby cutting cost on tape
  • Hangar Shaped polybags to pack the garments which are shipped with hangers, unfolded
  • Lay flat tube (plastic sheet) in PP/LD for moisture proofing inside carton
  • Perforated polybags with permanent tape for pilfer proofing of the packed item.
  • Bio-oxy based and PLA Corn based Polybags
  • Biogedradable Polybags
  • Recyclable polybags upto 30% of recycled raw material to promote environment conservation
